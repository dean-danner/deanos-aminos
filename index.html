Deano's Aminos!

Modes

Quiz Mode: Help

You have 60 seconds to match an amino acid identifier to its corresponding identifier. You are awarded points based off how fast you are able to answer the question correctly for up to 5 points per question. There are 20 questions.

Study Mode: Help

A flashcard with an amino acid structure is displayed. You can click the flashcard to show its corresponding name and info. There are 20 flashcards and the order is randomized.
